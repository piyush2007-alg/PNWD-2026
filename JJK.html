Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Gege Akutami. The animation is produced by MAPPA studio. It features intense battles, powerful sorcerers, and dangerous curses.

About the Animation

  • Studio: MAPPA
  • Genre: Action, Supernatural, Dark Fantasy
  • First Released: 2020
  • High-quality fight choreography

Main Characters

  1. Yuji Itadori
  2. Megumi Fushiguro
  3. Nobara Kugisaki
  4. Satoru Gojo
  5. Ryomen Sukuna
